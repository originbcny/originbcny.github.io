Services

Graphic Design, Illustrator, Photoshop, Web Design

BC offers a wide range of service for the business minded entrepreneur, launching or running his own brand, or business. To the starving artist needing exposure or trying to enter new fields for growth. BC motto is simple BecomeCreative,... Using up to date programs and old knowledge wizardry, we can craft a simple sketch, design or doodle to a finish clean graphic, ready to print on your favorite apparel. Already have uploaded custom graphics? Dont know what to do with them?... Perhaps premium glossy sticker packs to give out... BC uses UV/waterproof adhesive vinyl with glossy clear laminate for long lasting professional materials that will not peel or fade indoor or out. Custom layout for advertising, marketing or promotion, will add that professional look to your line, but unique to your style. BC does not use any templates, or generic plug in layouts. Each job is tailored to fit your style, theme and appearance.

BC helps any artist create a professional brand and seamless product line based off their portfolio of work and meaning. Turn paper into fashion by using our illustrator service to digitally recreate your art for the launch of your new clothing line. Need artist for a continued clothing line? BC team of professional artist can create custom work for any company, start-up or freelance.

Photoshop

Pictures from photo shoot came out too dark, or notice dust and lint ? Our graphic design department can edit any photo for resizing, optimization for web, Image optimization - reduces image size for faster web page loading time.

Photo retouching - professional photo retouching and editing to remove artifacts missed during photo shoot. The final step to professional clean image, product line, or portfolio

Photo/image creation or manipulation - Collage work turning your personal photo gallery into a creative collage ready to print on your medium of choose... rendering personal sketches to full color and depth,

Illustrator

Take your illustrations beyond ink and paper, by turning your work to vector art. Advantages in vector art allow for clean even line work. Resizable image without blur or image distortion. Allows for screen print use and multi color or pattern for sticker or label options. Turn your illustrations into vector recreation for screen print or digital copies in launching you brand. Create a professional comic book based off your work.

Web Design

In todays online world, having a working website is a must, but having a custom tailored website maybe difficult if not costly. Translate your theme into a functioning website that will have a clear representation of your brand image...

Turn your web template into a personal design suited for your website, We do not sell templates but offer customization to any downloaded or purchased theme you have.
